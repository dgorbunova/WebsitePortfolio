My backgorund

My name is Daria Gorbunova, but I also go by Dasha. I am 19 years old and am originally from Russia, but I have been studying in the United Kingdom for the past 9 years. I am from a small industrial city called Nizhnevartovsk, located in western Siberia. I moved to the UK in 2015 to study at a boarding school in Eastbourne. I then moved to Kingswood School in Bath, where I did my GCSEs and A-Levels.

My interests

I have always enjoyed programming during my time at school, but I've also been interested in lighting design, which led me to take an A-Level in Drama and Theatre Studies. Doing this allowed me to explore not only the creative side of stage lighting, but also technical which involved programming lighting boards, rigging and electrical aspects. On top of this I also enjoy running and cycling in my free time.
